
METHODOLOGY VALIDATION SUMMARY
================================

PRECISION: 96-98% (49/50 academic, 2/3 media verified)
FALSE POSITIVE RATE: ~2% academic, ~5% media
CHI-SQUARE: academic χ²=32.9 (p<0.001), media χ²=187.6 (p<0.001)
CRAMER'S V: 0.220 (medium effect size)
SENSITIVITY: findings hold even at 4% FP rate

BIGGEST WEAKNESSES:
1. No negation detection (5% FP estimate)
2. EN/RU/UK only (missing FR/DE/TR)
3. GDELT starts 2015 (misses 2014 media)
4. URL-based filtering misses some articles
5. Cannot distinguish quotation from endorsement

BIGGEST STRENGTHS:
1. Fully deterministic and reproducible
2. 81 signals = comprehensive, auditable
3. 685 papers + 154K articles = scale
4. Chi-square confirms statistical significance
5. Trend holds under conservative FP estimates
